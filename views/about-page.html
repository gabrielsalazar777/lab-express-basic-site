About Sergio Ramos

Sergio Ramos is a widely-renowned Spanish defender who enjoyed the peak of his career whilst playing for Spanish giants Real Madrid. Ramos is known for an aggressive defending style and strong loyalty towards his teammates.

As a consistent starter and long-time captain, he held the back line on his way to winning multiple domestic and international club tournaments for Real Madrid.

Sergio Ramos accepted a transfer to French club Paris-Saint Germain in 2021 and has been enjoying the twlight of his career playing French football ever since.

Born in 1986 in Camas, Spain, Ramos is now married to famed Spanish journalist Pilar Rubio and the couple share four sons. In 2014, he was named as a UNICEF Spain ambassador.

To learn more about Sergio, one can watch his 2019 Amazon documentary titled 'El Corazon de Sergio Ramos', which delves into his professional and personal life.
