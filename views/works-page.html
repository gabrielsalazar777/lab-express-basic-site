Sergio Ramos' Works

Sergio Ramos has many career achievements and is considered one of the best defenders of this generation. During his 16 seasons with Spanish club Real Madrid and the Spanish National Team, as well as 2 seasons with French club Paris-Saint Germain, Ramos has won a total of 27 club and international championships.

Some of Sergio's most notable achievements include:

  • 1x World Cup Champion (2010)
  • 2x European Champion (2008, 2012)
  • 4x Champions League winner (2014, 2016-2018)
  • 5x Spanish League winner (2007-2008, 2012, 2017, 2020)
  • 6x Defender of the Year (2012-2015, 2017-2018)
